How It Works

"We need to raise money" to "funds hit your account" in 3 steps.

01

Set Up Your Tournament

Pick a theme your school will love: sports, pop culture, school history. Set a prize, choose your fundraising goal, and invite your team or club as hosts.

02

Your School Community Enters

Share your competition link with students, parents, and staff. They pay a small entry fee, answer the trivia questions, and battle for the top of the leaderboard.

03

Your Group Gets Funded

The top scorer wins the prize. Entry fees go straight to your team or club's fundraising pot. Hit your goal and put the money toward what matters — new kit, a trip, or whatever your group needs.

The smart fundraising choice

Why Trivia?

Bake sales and car washes are fine. But this is way more fun (and raises more money).

More Engaging

Students, parents, and staff are far more likely to give when there's fun, friendly competition involved. A trivia tournament beats a "please donate" ask every time.

Built to Share

Competitions spread fast through school hallways, group chats, and parent networks. More entries mean a bigger pot — and more school spirit along the way.

Real Prizes, Real Incentive

A tangible prize dramatically increases participation. When students have a shot at winning something, they enter — and they tell their friends to enter too.

Funds Go to Your Group

Every entry fee (minus payment processing costs) goes directly to your team or club. No hidden platform cut. Full transparency — school admins and boosters included.
